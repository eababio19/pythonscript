Welcome to the Akan Names Generator!

Discover your unique Akan name and its meaning by inputting your date of birth and gender. Immerse yourself in Ghanaian culture through this delightful experience.

How It Works:

  • Input Your Details: Provide your date of birth (in YYYY-MM-DD format) and select your gender.
  • Generate Akan Name: Our app calculates your Akan name based on the day of the week you were born and your gender.
  • Uncover the Meaning: Dive deeper into Ghanaian culture by discovering the meaning behind your Akan name.

Why Akan Names?

Akan names are more than just identifiers; they carry cultural significance tied to the day of the week you were born. Immerse yourself in the beauty of Ghanaian traditions with every generated name.
